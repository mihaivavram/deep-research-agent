Search software review sites for: $ARGUMENTS

Use WebSearch across G2, Capterra, and Trustpilot to find reviews for relevant products. Run separate queries for each:
- `site:g2.com $ARGUMENTS`
- `site:capterra.com $ARGUMENTS`
- `site:trustpilot.com $ARGUMENTS`

For the top 2–3 results per site, use WebFetch to extract:
- Overall rating and review count (scale signal)
- Star distribution (% of 1-star vs 5-star reveals satisfaction shape)
- Most common praise themes (what the product does well)
- Most common complaint themes (positioning gaps competitors can exploit)
- Use case tags and buyer segments (who is actually using it)
- Competitor alternatives mentioned in reviews

On G2, also look at the comparison pages (`/compare/`) — these structured side-by-sides are gold for competitive positioning.
