Search for funding and company intelligence on: $ARGUMENTS

Use a combination of sources to build a funding and competitive landscape picture:

**Crunchbase (primary):**
Use WebSearch with `site:crunchbase.com $ARGUMENTS` to find company profiles. Use WebFetch on the top results to extract:
- Total funding raised and funding rounds (series, amount, date)
- Key investors (repeat investors across competitors signal category conviction)
- Founding year and employee headcount range
- Recent acquisitions or acqui-hires

**TechCrunch / VentureBeat (funding news):**
Use WebSearch for `"$ARGUMENTS" funding site:techcrunch.com OR site:venturebeat.com` to find funding announcements with richer context than Crunchbase alone.

**LinkedIn (hiring as strategy signal):**
Use WebSearch for `site:linkedin.com/jobs $ARGUMENTS` to find open roles. A company's active job postings reveal strategic priorities — heavy ML hiring signals a product direction before any press release does.

**Pitchbook coverage:**
Search `site:pitchbook.com $ARGUMENTS` for any publicly available market data or reports.

Return: companies found, funding totals and rounds, key investors, headcount signals, hiring themes, and strategic inferences, with source URLs.
