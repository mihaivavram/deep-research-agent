- Overall rating and breakdown (culture, management, compensation, work-life balance)
- Most common pros and cons in reviews (operational health signal)
- CEO approval rating and trend

**LinkedIn Job Postings (strategy as a leading indicator):**
Use WebSearch for `site:linkedin.com/jobs "$ARGUMENTS"` and related searches. Job postings reveal strategic bets before press releases do:
- Heavy ML/AI engineer hiring → building AI-native features
- Lots of sales/SDR roles → aggressive growth push
- Customer success hires → post-sale retention problems or expansion motion
- New market-specific roles (e.g. "EMEA Account Executive") → geographic expansion
