**Review analysis:**
- Overall star rating and total review count (market size + satisfaction signal)
- Star distribution — a high volume of 3-star reviews often signals "good enough but missing X"
- Filter for critical reviews (1–2 star): recurring complaints reveal unmet needs
- Filter for recent reviews to surface current sentiment vs. legacy reputation
- Common phrases in review text (verbatim language customers use is copywriting gold)

**Product signals:**
- "Frequently bought together" and "Customers also viewed" (competitive set)
- Q&A section — unanswered questions reveal friction and feature gaps
- Seller and pricing info (market structure, margin environment)
